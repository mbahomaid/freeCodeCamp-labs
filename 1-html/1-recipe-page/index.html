Ichiraku Ramen

Inspired by the beloved ramen from the anime Naruto, Ichiraku Ramen is a comforting and flavorful dish that’s perfect for any ramen lover. This recipe captures the essence of the iconic bowl enjoyed by Naruto Uzumaki himself—featuring a rich, savory broth, springy noodles, and classic toppings. Let’s bring a taste of the Hidden Leaf Village to your kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 green onion, chopped

Instructions

  1. Heat sesame oil in a pot, sauté garlic, ginger, and green onions, then add beef broth, water, soy sauce, and miso paste, letting it simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  2. Cook the beef slices in a separate pan over medium heat until tender, then set aside.
  3. Boil ramen noodles as per package instructions, drain, and cook eggs to desired doneness before peeling and slicing.
  4. Place cooked noodles in bowls, pour the hot broth over them, and add beef, eggs, corn, mushrooms, bok choy, and nori.
  5. Garnish with toasted sesame seeds, mix everything together, and enjoy your homemade beef ramen!
